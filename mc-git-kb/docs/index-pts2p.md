## "Samurai Mindset"
| What | Why |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Pain is not the enemy** | - Pain is part of the price. Discomfort is the cost of growth.<br>- Doing the thing, even when it's hard; that's where strength is built. |
| **Focus is your sword** | - If your mind is scattered, you’ve already lost.<br>- Cut distractions. Protect your attention like it’s your edge. |
| **Discipline > Motivation** | - Motivation fades; discipline shows up without permission.<br>- Set routines that don't rely on how you feel. |
| **Train without a crowd** | - Real growth happens when no one is watching.<br>- Do the work when no one's watching. That's how you earn your edge. |
| **Master yourself first** | - Control your impulses, or your impulses will control your outcomes.<br>- Track what triggers you and learn to pause before reacting. |
| **Think long, act daily** | - Meaningful change doesn't happen overnight.<br>- Stay patient with the timeline and strict with the routine. |
| **Stay sharp in peacetime** | - Comfort is where most people lose the battle.<br>- Keep improving even when things feel easy. |
