## BSA Certifications

#### IIBA 
*International Institute of Business Analysts*
  - **ECBA** :: Entry Certificate in Business Analysis
  - **CCBA** :: Certification of Competency in Business Analysis
  - **CBAP** :: Certified Business Analysis Professional

#### PMI-PBA 
*Project Management Institute Professional in Business Analysis*

#### CSPO
*Certified Scrum Product Owner*

#### PSPO
*Professional Scrum Product Owner*

#### CPRE
*Certified Professional for Requirements Engineering*
  - IREB (International Requirements Engineering Board)
  - Highly specialized certification focusing purely on requirements engineering and management. Excellent for BAs in complex technical domains.
