## Principles of Kaizen
**#DevelopGoodHabits.com**<br>
<small>*Try to do something just a little bit better each day in order to make a larger impact in the long run*</small>

1. Never Stop
  <br>*There is an area for improvement in everyone's life, no matter how successful they are*
1. Eliminate Old Practices
  <br>*Let go of former habits, even if they seemed to have some benefit to you at the time*
1. Be Proactive
  <br>*Stop hesitating because of possible hurdles that you foresee and move forward*
1. Don't Assume New Methods Will Work
  <br>*Just because something worked for someone else does not mean that it will work for you*
1. Make Corrections
  <br>*Recognize areas that can use improvement and make relevant corrections*
1. Empower All Employees to Speak Up
  <br>*Encourage suggestions from others if they see things that could be worked on*
1. Crowdsource
  <br>*Learn from other people and get some new ideas on things you can use to create continuous improvement*
1. Practice the "5 Whys" Method
  <br>*Continuing to ask why can help to find the real reason behind a problem*
  <br>***NOTE***: Worked with someone who said 3 should be sufficient; otherwise, get help
1. Be Economical
  <br>*Consider specific things you can save money on*
1. Don't Stop
  <br>*Never consider yourself to be finished*
  <br>*Improvement has no limits*
